# Code of Conduct

SuperNova welcomes contributors who want to build open coordination tools for humans, AI agents, organizations, and communities with care.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risks.
- Keep safety and privacy concerns grounded in specific behavior.
- Preserve the project's non-financial, public-interest, manual-preview governance posture unless a dedicated review changes it.
- Help newcomers understand active paths, protected core paths, and current guardrails.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private user data, secrets, tokens, direct messages, or exploit details.
- Pressuring maintainers to merge risky execution, federation-write, auth, or DB changes without review.
- Attempting to bypass safety checks, protected-core rules, or public-export privacy boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block contributors; or pause discussions that put people, data, infrastructure, or the project's safety posture at risk.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact maintainers through the least public available project channel. For security-sensitive conduct or exploit details, follow `SECURITY.md`.
